# Animal Sorting Challenge

Sort the animals **by their Weight (kg)**, from **smallest → largest**.

## Animals

- **Western Lowland Gorilla**
- **Water Buffalo**
- **Galápagos Penguin**
- **Snow Leopard**
- **Yellow-Eyed Penguin**

## Correct Order

Yellow-Eyed Penguin → Galápagos Penguin → Snow Leopard → Western Lowland Gorilla → Water Buffalo
