# Animal Sorting Challenge

Sort the animals **by their Weight (kg)**, from **smallest → largest**.

## Animals

- **Star-Nosed Mole**
- **Galápagos Tortoise**
- **Sun Bear**
- **Giant Pacific Octopus**
- **Water Buffalo**

## Correct Order

Star-Nosed Mole → Giant Pacific Octopus → Sun Bear → Galápagos Tortoise → Water Buffalo
