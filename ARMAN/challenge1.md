# Animal Sorting Challenge

Sort the animals **by their Lifespan (years)**, from **smallest → largest**.

## Animals

- **Cheetah**
- **Brown Bear**
- **Golden Poison Dart Frog**
- **Star-Nosed Mole**
- **Platypus**

## Correct Order

Star-Nosed Mole → Cheetah → Golden Poison Dart Frog → Platypus → Brown Bear
