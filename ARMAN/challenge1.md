# Animal Sorting Challenge

Sort the animals **by their Average Speed (km/h)**, from **smallest → largest**.

## Animals

- **Star-Nosed Mole**
- **Arabian Oryx**
- **Vulture**
- **Bottlenose Dolphin**
- **Kiwi**

## Correct Order

Star-Nosed Mole → Arabian Oryx → Vulture → Kiwi → Bottlenose Dolphin
