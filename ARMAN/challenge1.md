# Animal Sorting Challenge

Sort the animals **by their Gestation Period (days)**, from **smallest → largest**.

## Animals

- **Amazon Rainforest Frog**
- **Galápagos Tortoise**
- **Kiwi**
- **Snow Leopard**
- **Mountain Gorilla**

## Correct Order

Amazon Rainforest Frog → Kiwi → Snow Leopard → Galápagos Tortoise → Mountain Gorilla
