# Animal Sorting Challenge

Sort the animals **by their Height (cm)**, from **smallest → largest**.

## Animals

- **Star-Nosed Mole**
- **Water Buffalo**
- **Bottlenose Dolphin**
- **African Elephant**
- **Snow Leopard**

## Correct Order

Star-Nosed Mole → Snow Leopard → Water Buffalo → African Elephant → Bottlenose Dolphin
