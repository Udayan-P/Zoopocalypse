# Animal Sorting Challenge

Sort the animals **by their Average Speed (km/h)**, from **smallest → largest**.

## Animals

- **Brown Bear**
- **Kiwi**
- **African Elephant**
- **Western Lowland Gorilla**
- **Wild Boar**

## Correct Order

Kiwi → Wild Boar → African Elephant → Western Lowland Gorilla → Brown Bear
