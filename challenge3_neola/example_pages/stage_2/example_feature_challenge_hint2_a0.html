Feature Challenge: Identify the Animal

Test your knowledge by revealing hints and guessing the species.

How the Game Works

• You begin with 5 initial revealed hints based on the species’ attributes.
• You may unlock up to 3 additional hints, one at a time.
• You have a total of 2 wrong attempts to guess the correct species.
• If you reach 2 incorrect guesses, the challenge ends and the final species is revealed.
• Once all hints are shown or the answer is revealed, all hidden attributes become visible.
• You also have access to one AI-generated hint, which remains consistent throughout the challenge.
